About me

Hongjun Lim

Machine Learning Research Engineer

My name is Hongjun Lim. I studied engineering in Korea at the Ajou university and got MS degree at KAIST. I`m currently a machine learning engineer at Naver Search, working in the AiRSearch (AI Recommendation and Search) team with a focus on building scalable recommender systems for real-world problems. My interests lie at the intersection of applied machine learning, information retrieval and natural language processing with a current emphasis on user modeling for personalized news recommendation.
